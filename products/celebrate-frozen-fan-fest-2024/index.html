Snow Many Ways to Celebrate Frozen Fan Fest 2024

Published: October 4, 2024

Contributed By

Caleb Carden

Communications Specialist, Disney Consumer Products

Hear ye, hear ye – it’s time for a celebration! No, it’s not Coronation Day – it’s the return of Frozen Fan Fest! This month, we’re celebrating all things Frozen, and I can hardly wait.

We are headed back to the magical world of Frozen as we get ready to embark on new icy adventures alongside Anna, Elsa, Olaf and their friends with new Frozen content and products launching this month.

To kick off the celebration, we have a flurry of products inspired by brand-new storytelling! Disney Off the Page Adventures: Arendelle Ice Calamity is a new Frozen story that comes to life in a storybook stylization, launching on the Disney Princess YouTube channel today, and reuniting us with Anna, Elsa, Olaf, and Kristoff in an icy adventure.

In this all-new Frozen story, it’s the day of Arendelle’s first big Winter Festival, and Anna and Elsa have high hopes for the inaugural event. But when an outright calamity threatens to ruin the day, Anna and Elsa must band together with Kristoff, Olaf and Sven to save the festivities. Thrown way out of their comfort zones, they use the power of family, friendship, and fearlessness to pull off the impossible.

And what’s really “cool” (get it) is that this story has inspired the creation of new products including the Anna & Elsa Ice Skating Fashion Dolls and Olaf’s Treats Sled Playset. 

And that’s not all! Disney Frozen: Winter Festival is a new stop-motion creation that will also debut on the Disney Princess YouTube channel. Episode One will be available beginning October 7, with additional episodes dropping weekly on YouTube throughout the month.

In this 4-episode series, Anna and Elsa are excited to host the inaugural Winter Festival in Arendelle celebrating the beginning of winter and all the fun festivities the season brings! The sisters and their friends spread out to prepare for the festival, drumming up excitement around town, setting up for the sporting events, and sharing warm treats. Will Elsa make it back in time? Will icy magic transform the whole town square into an ice-skating extravaganza? And who will take home the Winter Festival Trophy? 

If all this Frozen fun is inspiring you to recreate a Winter Festival of your own, the all-new products inspired by this brand-new storytelling can help bring the magical world of Frozen right to your home! Plus, keep reading for even more of our Frozen favorites to help you celebrate Frozen Fan Fest all month long.

Dream up endless wintery games with the Ice-Skating Anna and Elsa Fashion Dolls, each dressed for the Winter Festival and ready for ice skating fun! The Celebration Anna and Elsa Fashion Dolls wear removable dresses inspired by their Winter Festival gowns and detachable capes with touches of fluffy trim for the snowy season.

Kids can let their sense of adventure ride with the Elsa & Nokk Adventure Carriage, featuring the mythical horse-shaped water spirit, the Nokk, who carries a magical, iridescent carriage with working wheels. Or, you can help drum up excitement for the Winter Festival by passing out tasty treats with Olaf! The Olaf Treat Sled Playset comes with hot cocoa mugs, cookies, and more for all of your friends in Arendelle.

Keep the Frozen fun going with even more of our Frozen favorites including the new Icy Magic Collection by Jakks Pacific. Get ready for an icy tea party with the Icy Magic Tea Set, or style yourself for your next journey with the Icy Magic Vanity. Imagine you have Elsa’s icy powers as you activate the icy magic of these fun playsets that light up like blue “ice” and make “freezing” ice crackling sounds. Elsa’s Icy Magic Musical Dress features shimmering snowflakes and ice crystal designs, and lights up and plays “Let It Go” when you press the snowflake. You can also harness the power of snow magic with the Icy Magic Wand, inspired by Elsa’s icy powers.

The Disney Frozen Anna and Elsa Collector Dolls by American Girl are perfect for collectors of any age! Each doll comes in their signature look from Disney’s Frozen and is bedazzled with Swarovski crystals. Elsa’s Ice Palace isn’t the only thing shimmering here! Speaking of Elsa’s Ice Palace, unlimited story and play opportunities await as kids build a Frozen world of their own with LEGO Elsa’s Ice Palace, available on Disney Store, LEGO.com, and while supplies last at LEGO Store Downtown Disney and Disney Springs.

Halloween is just around the corner, and whether they’re getting ready for trick-or-treating or for imaginative playtime, kids will love these Anna, Elsa, and Olaf costumes by Disguise, inspired by Disney’s Frozen 2. 

Speaking of dressing up, Frozen fans can complete their ‘fit with these must-haves from Disney Store, inspired by our favorite loveable snowman. Tiny toes will feel warm as summer (Olaf’s favorite time of year) with these fleecy Olaf Clogs for Kids by Crocs, decorated like Olaf’s face with a 3-D soft carrot nose. And, you’ll melt hearts in this cool-as-ice Olaf Baseball Cap for Adults with plush carrot nose, felt hair and padded tooth. To complete your collection, add the Anna and Elsa Disney nuiMOs Plush as the perfect cute and cuddly companions.

Bring the charm of Arendelle to your wrist with the Disney Frozen Box Set from Citizen, complete with a dazzling timepiece and special pin of a snowflake. The Frozen watch features a silver-tone stainless steel case and matching polished bracelet, channeling a snowy design inspired by the fearless Elsa. The azure dial is decorated with cool blues and a delicate snowflake pattern reminiscent of Elsa’s ice palace, with crystals shimmering like ice for each hour. Also, add a little Frozen magic to yourPandora jewelry with the Disney Parks World of Frozen Charm, available at store locations within Disney Parks & Resorts as well as at Pandora Disney Springs & Pandora Downtown Disney District.

Audible and Disney Publishing have released Disney Frozen: Olaf’s Quest, a new Audible Original featuring Disney character voices and immersive sound design. Olaf, Arendelle’s adorable snowman, loves to read. One day, an unexpected message leads Olaf and his friends Anna, Kristoff, and Sven on a quest for a place known as Haus Biblio, described as a “sea of stories.” Join the group’s journey as they cross over mountains, search for monsters under bridges, and have conversations with friendly sheep.

And speaking of that adorable snowman, Olaf has arrived in Disney Frozen Royal Castle, where you can dress up, cook, and explore in your very own royal doll house! Play with Anna, Elsa, Kristoff and other Frozen dolls in this fun mobile app. Download the new mobile app for free, with in-app purchases, on Google Play, App Store, or Amazon App Store today!

As you can see, there are so many ways to celebrate Frozen Fan Fest this month and beyond – and there’s even more still to come! Stay tuned for all-new Frozen apparel, accessories, plush and more coming to Disney Parks this December. ‘Til then, warm hugs!
