Unwrap the Magic: D23 Event Merchandise Now Available at DisneyStore.com! 

Published: August 12, 2024

Contributed By

Kelly Ann Vasquez

Public Relations Intern, Disney Consumer Products

D23: The Ultimate Disney Fan Event may have come to a close, but the magic is far from over! If you missed the event or want to relive the enchantment, we’re thrilled to announce that many of the products showcased at the event are now available online. Dive into the magic of Disney with these must-have items, all now live on DisneyStore.com! 

Heroes and Villains Collection 

Discover the ultimate showdown between good and evil with our collection themed after heroes and villains! The Disney Princess Heroes and Villains MagicBand+ is a limited-edition must-have that’s perfect for any park day, while the Maleficent Ear Headband and Maleficent Backpack add a touch of villainous charm to any outfit. Plush lovers will adore the Disney Parks Wishables Good Vs. Evil Plush Blind Pack and the Ariel Disney Parks Wishables from Disney’s “The Little Mermaid.” 

Vinyl collectors, prepare yourselves! The Stacey Aoyama Disney Designer Showcase features exquisite Disney vinyl sets, including Ariel and Ursula from “The Little Mermaid,” Hercules and Hades from “Hercules,” Jasmine and Jafar from “Aladdin,” Snow White and the Evil Queen from “Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs,” and Briar Rose and Maleficent from “Sleeping Beauty.” Each set captures the essence of these beloved characters with stunning detail and is a timeless treasure for Disney collectors and enthusiasts alike. 

Midnight Masquerade: Fashionably Late 

Unmask the magic of the Midnight Masquerade collection, where elegance and whimsy collide in an extraordinary display. The newest Midnight Masquerade Box Set showcases Disney’s Gaston, Queen of Hearts, Alice, Mother Gothel, Ariel, and Snow White, offering a treasure trove of elegance. These six aren’t the only ones with an invitation in the mail! Collectors will not want to miss the limited-edition Aladdin & Jasmine and Anastasia & Drizella Doll Sets, which are unparalleled gems for any collection. 

​​Star Wars​ Must-Have​ Item​s 

The galaxy’s finest are here! Recently revealed and now available exclusively at DisneyStore.com, the limited-edition Legacy Lightsaber ​Hilt ​set three-pack celebrat​es​ beloved characters from Star Wars: The Clone Wars – Anakin Skywalker, Ahsoka Tano, and Obi-Wan Kenobi, alongside the Blade of Ochi of Bestoon collectible. Fans of Star Tours will not want to miss the limited-edition Starspeeder 3000, a Star Tours vehicle replica complete with original attraction footage. It also has light, sounds and a carded RX-24 Droid Factory figure among other great features. For those who lean towards the dark side​ of the Force​, the Kylo Ren Reforged Helmet is an artifact of unparalleled significance. Complete your collection with the Pit Droid Interactive Action Figure, R6-D23 Droid Factory Figure, and RX-24 Talking Action Figure.

Star Wars Dark Side​ Artist Series Collection​ 

Showcase your fandom with the Star Wars: ​​Dark Side Artist Series collection. This collection includes the Dark Side Spirit Jersey,​ a​ Loungefly Ear Headband, and​ a​ Loungefly Mini Backpack for adults, all featuring designs by Disney artist Alex Riegert-Waters​,​ showcasing iconic Star Wars villains. Take command of your Star Wars collection with the Darth Vader Figurine and the Dark Side Matted Print, using art designed by Riegert-Waters. You won’t want to miss adding these items ​to your collection​!​ 

Disney Munchlings Collection 

Snuggle up with the Disney Munchlings Baked Treats Remix! This delightful collection includes charming new additions like the Mystery Scented Plush – Baked Treats Remix, featuring Minnie Mouse Cherry Tart, Baymax Tiramisu, and my top pick, the Stitch Frosted Blueberry Loaf, all with delightful scents. Looking for something even cuddlier? Check out the larger Mickey Mouse Steamed Bun Willie Plush for an extra dose of plushy charm! 

Mary Poppins 60th Anniversary 

Celebrate the magic of Mary Poppins with the 60th Anniversary Loungefly Ear Headband and the Penguin Waiter Plush. Inspired by a jolly holiday, the Loungefly Ear Headband features charming details that evoke the whimsical spirit of the esteemed nanny, while the Penguin Waiter Plush brings a touch of cheer from the classic film. These delightful items are as sweet as a spoonful of sugar! 

Ichabod & Mr. Toad 75th Anniversary 

Mark the 75th anniversary of “The Adventures of Ichabod and Mr. Toad” with exclusive merchandise. The Loungefly Mini Backpack with Light-Up Belt Bag captures the adventurous spirit of the film, while the Ichabod Crane and Headless Horseman Light-Up Figure Set brings iconic moments to life with illuminating effects. Complement your look with the Ear Headband, featuring charming details from the movie, and stay cozy in the Varsity Jacket and Premium T-Shirt, both showcasing thematic designs. Complete your look with the elegant Pocket Watch by Bulova, a timeless piece that pays tribute to Mr. Toad’s character.  

Disney Pins: Joy on the Screen 

Pin collectors, get ready to elevate your collection with the Disney Pins: Joy on the Screen collection! This exquisite, limited-edition range celebrates the magic of beloved characters with unparalleled artistry and detail. The collection features a sketch series highlighting the artistic journey of characters such as Sorcerer Mickey Mouse, Ariel, and Simba. It includes a dedicated series with an Animation Desk Pin Set that brings to life timeless films such as ”Cinderella,” ”Sleeping Beauty,” and “Dumbo” and a Multiplane Camera Slider Pin featuring Snow White. Further enriching the collection is a movie ticket series that honors some favorites like “Alice in Wonderland,” “A Goofy Movie,” and “Fantasia 2000.” Adding a touch of romance, the jumbo pin set features beloved couples like Ariel and Eric, Belle and the Beast, and Aladdin and Jasmine. Lastly, don’t miss the deluxe Disney Animation and Imagineering Jumbo Pin, where Sorcerer Mickey Mouse and Tinker Bell present a collection of artists’ sketches, along with three animation model sheets and two castle layouts that honor the talented Disney Legends behind them. 

The Haunted Mansion 55th Anniversary 

Happy haunts have been materializing for 55 years at The Haunted Mansion at Disneyland Resort, and what better way to celebrate than with some hauntingly delightful items. Adorn your shelf with this Madame Leota Replica. For those who prefer to carry a touch of magic with them, the Raven Magnetic Shoulder Plush will have you grim and grinning. 

A Walk in Disneyland & Parks Logo Play 

Immerse yourself in the magic of Disneyland Park with the A Walk in Disneyland collection, featuring the elegant Disneyland Dress for Women, the charming Disneyland Teacup Set, and the stylish Disneyland Icons Woven Shirt for Adults, all crafted by Ashley Eckstein and Ashley Taylor. For a more casual, streetwear look, check out the Parks Logo Play collection, including the D23 Varsity Jacket and Baseball Cap. Both collections celebrate the Park’s spirit with nostalgic flair and modern style. 

Disney Lacers 

Step into Disney magic with every stride in Disney Lacers! Explore the exclusive limited-release vinyl collection, including the Disney Lacers Mystery Set – Series One, Lacers Star Wars Vinyl, Lacers Donald 90th Vinyl, and more. Each collectible is a unique tribute to timeless icons and beloved characters, adding a touch of enchantment to your Disney collection. 

15 Years of Pixar’s Up 

Celebrate Pixar’s magic with the Carl Fredricksen and Ellie Cake Topper and Cake Serving Set from Disney and Pixar’s “Up.” These items are perfect for adding a touch of adventure to any celebration. 

All these magical items are now available online and at the Disneyland Resort, so visit DisneyStore.com/D23Event2024 to shop and bring home the magic of D23: The Ultimate Disney Fan Event!
