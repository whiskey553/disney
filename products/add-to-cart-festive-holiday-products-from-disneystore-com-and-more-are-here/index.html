Add to Cart: Festive Holiday Products from DisneyStore.com and More Are Here!

Published: September 30, 2024

Contributed By

Kelly Ann Vasquez

Public Relations Intern, Disney Consumer Products

The holiday season is just around the corner, and there’s nothing like festive Disney goods to elevate the cheer in your home—whether you’re cozying up with family for a movie night, baking holiday treats, or trimming the tree!

The best news? The Disney Store Holiday Shop launches today and includes a delightful range of new holiday products to bring festive cheer to every part of your home and wardrobe. Let’s dive into key offerings from DisneyStore.com and other retailers to make your holidays merry and bright!

Cozy Winter Essentials 

Start your holiday season with cozy essentials that keep you warm and festive. Imagine waking up on a chilly morning, wrapped in the Mickey Mouse Plaid Holiday Sleep Set for Adults, perfect for staying stylish while lounging. Pair it with the Minnie Mouse Plaid Holiday Sleep Set for Women or outfit your little ones in the Minnie Mouse Plaid Holiday Sleep Set for Kids. Complete the look with the Mickey Mouse Holiday Slippers for Adults and Mickey Mouse Holiday Slippers for Kids, ideal for a family photo-op in front of the tree. 

If you’re heading outdoors, the Santa Mickey Mouse Holiday Zip Fleece Jacket for Adults brings a festive flair to your outfit. For a cozy yet fashionable option indoors or out, the Mickey Mouse and Friends Fantasyland Castle Holiday Zip Hoodie for Women is a perfect choice. Pair these with the Mickey and Minnie Mouse Holiday Clogs for Adults by Crocs or Mickey and Minnie Mouse Holiday Clogs for Kids by Crocs to complete your winter ensemble. And don’t forget to dress up your little ones in the adorable Minnie Mouse Holiday Dress Set for Baby and other Infant Santa Mickey Sets for a picture-perfect holiday celebration. 

Jolly Apparel & Accessories  

Complete your festive look with the Disney Dogs “Santa Paws” Dooney & Bourke, featuring a tote bag, crossbody bag, and wallet that add a touch of sophistication to your holiday outfits. For a fun and festive option, check out the Mickey Mouse Icon Holiday Spirit Jersey for Adults and the Mickey Mouse Holiday Lights Loungefly Mini Backpack, perfect for carrying your essentials while spreading holiday cheer.

For even more festive apparel, BoxLunch offers a variety of cozy holiday sweaters, including the Super Hero-inspired Avengers Holiday Mashup Sweatshirt and the Ewok’n Around the Christmas Tree Sweater; perfect for the holidays and beyond. Adding a touch of magic, the Disney Beauty and the Beast Enchanted Rose Patterned Holiday Sweater features the iconic enchanted rose adorned in 3D velvet surrounded by silhouettes of Cogsworth and Lumière, perfect for bringing a little “tale as old as time” charm to your festivities. After saving the day, relax in the Baymax with Mochi Holiday Sweater, featuring everyone’s favorite healthcare companion and his trusty feline friend, Mochi, decked out in festive attire for the season. 

Holiday Baking and Dining 

Transform your kitchen into a holiday haven with Disney-themed baking and dining accessories. Slip on the charming Minnie Mouse Holiday Apron for Adults as you bake festive treats. Use the Mickey Mouse and Minnie Mouse Christmas Tree Stacking Measuring Cup Set and Mickey Mouse and Friends Holiday Cookie Stamp Set to add a touch of Disney magic to your cookies—then store your creations in the adorable Mickey Mouse Holiday Cookie Jar. When it’s time to serve, impress your guests with the Mickey Mouse and Friends Holiday Plate Set and the elegant Mickey Mouse and Friends Holiday Serving Platter. 

Decorations to Adorn Your Tree and Home 

Deck the halls with Disney decorations from Disney and more. Adorn your tree with the Mickey Mouse Sketchbook Ornament and Minnie Mouse Sketchbook Ornament, capturing the charm of these beloved characters. For an extra sparkle, the Mickey Mouse Icon Sleeping Beauty Castle Holiday Glass Sketchbook Ornament will make your tree shine. Place the Belle 2024 Holiday Special Edition Doll – Beauty and the Beast next to your tree for a delightful touch.

Enhance your festive display with the Rey and Kylo Ren Sketchbook Ornament based on – Star Wars: The Last Jedi, the Loki Holiday Light-Up Living Magic Sketchbook Ornament, the Ariel Light-Up Living Magic Sketchbook Ornament, and Pixar’s Carl, Russell, and Dug Sketchbook Ornament – Up, each adding unique and enchanting touches to your space.

For outdoor décor, the Airblown Disney Mickey Snowman by Gemmy stands 3.5 feet tall and adds a festive touch to your yard. Inside, the Star Wars Holiday Organic Sheet Set, complete with the quilt, shams, and matching throw pillow, transforms your bedroom into a cozy holiday retreat. 

Festive Fun with Games, Plushies, and Treats 

Bring extra joy to your holiday season with a mix of festive beverages, puzzles, and plushies. Sip on Disney-themed hot cocoa from Joffrey’s, like the Disney Mickey and Minnie Apres Ski Hot Cocoa, or enhance your coffee experience with International Delight’s Home Alone Hot Chocolate Marshmallow Creamer, Frosted Sugar Cookie Creamer, and Peppermint Mocha options. As you relax with your favorite drinks, enjoy quality family time piecing together Mickey’s Gingerbread House – 500 piece puzzle from Ceaco. For added coziness, snuggle up with the Squishmallows Original 8-Inch Santa Stitch from Jazwares, a perfect companion for the holidays. 

Collector’s Corner 

Expand your collection with special holiday-themed collectibles. The American Girl Disney Frozen Elsa Collector Doll and American Girl Disney Frozen Anna Collector Doll make exquisite additions for any Disney enthusiast. Funko POP! fans will adore the Disney Holiday Mickey Mouse Funko POP! Vinyl Figure and the Funko POP! Lilo and Stitch: Stitch with Lights. For those who can’t get enough of classic Holiday movies, the Home Alone Kevin with McCallister Home Funko POP! is a must. Super Hero fans will also love adding POP! Captain America with Wreath Shield from Funko to their collection – this super soldier has definitely gotten into the spirit of the season. Plus, Funko’s Pop! Red Hulk with Snowman is here to warm your heart with some holiday cheer!

Advent Calendars and Countdowns 

Count down the holiday season with themed advent calendars. Open the tiny doors on Funko’s Pocket Pop! Pixar 24-Day Holiday Advent Calendar to reveal some of your favorite Pixar characters! The Pocket Pop! Marvel 12-Day Countdown Calendar from Funko will put you in the holiday spirit with some of your favorite Super Heroes, while The LEGO Group’s LEGO Disney Advent Calendar 2024 offers daily builds and surprises. For a Star Wars twist, the Star Wars Joy to the Galaxy 12 Day Holiday Advent Calendar from LovePop is sure to delight fans of all ages.

As you prepare for this joyful time, these holiday items are sure to add a dash of yuletide magic and cheer to your celebrations. Whether you’re treating yourself or searching for the perfect gifts for loved ones, these treasures will create unforgettable memories. Want to shop in person? Don’t fret! You can find select Disney Store products at Disney Parks this holiday season, allowing you to shop while enjoying the festive atmosphere beginning this November! Get ready to deck the halls and embrace the most wonderful time of the year—Disney style!
