Disney Paper Parks: Happy Haunts Edition Designed by Walt Disney Imagineering, Part 8 

Published: October 28, 2024

Contributed By

Kevin Rafferty

Communications Manager

Disney Paper Parks is back for the Halloween season with this release of downloadable and printable 3D activity sheets inspired by The Haunted Mansion Parlor, the soon-to-debut lounge onboard Disney Cruise Line’s newest ship, the Disney Treasure.

The Happy Haunts Edition Part 8 Sheets Include:

  • Parlor Entry
  • Main Parlor Area
  • Furniture, Including Armchair and Cabinet

Plans for The Haunted Mansion Parlor will honor more than five decades of Haunted Mansion fandom and introduce the world to a new chapter, with the nostalgia of the original attraction alongside a new nautical storyline.

The venue will resemble a well-appointed ship parlor, which once belonged to a jovial sea captain and his strange, but captivating fiancée.

Build the Haunted Mansion Parlor Model

For best results, we recommend printing the activity sheets at their actual size, not scaling them to fit the page. We also recommend adult participation or supervision for those with little ones to help make this great activity even more enjoyable.
