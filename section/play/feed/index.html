We know you are excited to see these wallpapers, but first some facts about the history of DOLE Whip:  

  • The original name for DOLE Whip was DOLE Pineapple Whip. 
  • In 1984, DOLE Whip was introduced at Aloha Isle at Magic Kingdom Park. 
  • In 1986, DOLE Whip arrived at Disneyland Park at the Tiki Juice Bar. 
  • In 2014, Pineapple Lanai opened at Disney’s Polynesian Village Resort, making it the first time DOLE Whip was sold outside of the parks. 
  • In 2017, DOLE Whip began sailing the seas with Disney Cruise Line. 
  • In 2022, Swirls on the Water at Disney Springs opened, marking the first time a DOLE Whip sampler was offered at Walt Disney World Resort.  
  • In 2023, DOLE Whip Day officially became recognized as a national holiday to be celebrated on the third Thursday in July.
