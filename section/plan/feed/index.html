So, you are planning a Disney vacation, and the excitement is building among your family. What to pack, where are we staying, and oh yes, the most popular question of all … how many more days until Disney?!  

We get it and just like you, we here at planDisney can’t wait for our next Disney adventure. Whether you’re booking dining reservations 60 days before your trip or if your visit is just around the corner and it’s time to book Lightning Lanes, there are so many ways to count down to your Disney vacation. We’re always here to provide tips on how to stay, save and play at Walt Disney World, but have you ever thought of the many ways you can count down to that magical vacation? planDisney has, and we are here today to share some of our most creative ideas for counting down to your next magical Disney vacation. 

Tip #1: Use The Disney Countdown Clock Or Create Your Own 

Wondering just how many days are left before your next Disney trip? Amanda D. is always thinking about her next trip and reminds guests that they can use the Disney x Citizen countdown clock in the My Disney Experience and Disneyland apps to count the days until their hotel stays at Walt Disney World and Disneyland Resort. She also loves that the app shows artwork specific to the resort she’s staying at on either coast. And let’s not forget that there is also a countdown app for Disney Cruise Line guests as well. “The trackers for land-based vacations feature the resort you’ll be visiting, and they’re really fun. Wherever you’re heading, there’s nothing as exciting as seeing those countdowns approach zero!”

If you want to pass the time with a DIY project, create a paper chain like you might remember from school days. “Since our girls were small, we’ve always made a paper chain–usually featuring some Disney-themed paper—to count down to our next Disney adventure,” says Susan C. “It’s a fun way to involve kids in the planning! Even now that they’re teens, they still love to have our homemade countdown calendar to help us all get excited for our next trip Home!” 

Or if you’re visiting during the holiday season, add some festive flair, like Kristi R. does. “One of my favorite ways to build excitement for our holiday trips is to create a special countdown calendar (much like an Advent calendar) which reveals fun activities, dining reservations, and other little surprises to look forward to on our vacation. Our Elf has also been known to join the fun by delivering a new MagicBand+ or pair of Mickey ears direct from the North Pole!”    

Tip #2: Enjoy Themed Disney Movie Nights 

“We love to have Disney movie nights to countdown to our next Disney vacation, often theming them to a ride we are most excited about,” explains Matt N.  

“We may watch TRON: Legacy to get pumped for the ride at Magic Kingdom or The Princess and the Frog for Tiana’s Bayou Adventure. We may even have a themed treat to immerse us in the movie, too!” 

Tip #3: Remember and Share Old Disney Memories 

“Now that my daughters are adults and live in other states, our countdown takes a different approach. When we hit the 30-day mark, I start texting family photos of Disney Parks vacations to them from when they were young children,” explains Shannon D.  

“The closer we get to the trip, the more recent the photos. It is a perfect stroll down Main Street, U.S.A. memory lane as we count down the days to creating new magical memories!” 

For more great tips like these, the best ways to save on Disney vacations, our favorite Value Resorts, or for answers to your Disney vacation planning questions, visit us at planDisney.com and follow us at @planDisneyPanel on Instagram for more vacation planning tips and recommendations.

]]> 8 Hauntingly Great Tips for Celebrating Halloween at Walt Disney World   https://disneyparksblog.com/wdw/8-hauntingly-great-tips-for-celebrating-halloween-at-walt-disney-world/ Thu, 19 Sep 2024 16:01:17 +0000 https://disneyparksblog.com/?p=353290 Plan your spooky trip with these eight Halloween at Disney World tips to ensure you experience a frightful good time across the resort.

The post 8 Hauntingly Great Tips for Celebrating Halloween at Walt Disney World   appeared first on Disney Parks Blog.

]]>

If you’re planning a family vacation to Walt Disney World Resort during the Halloween season, you’ve come to the right place. The planDisney panelists are sharing 8 hauntingly great tips for experiencing frightfully fun offerings across the resort – from the theme parks to Disney Springs, and even at your Disney Resort hotel! 

Walt Disney World Theme Parks  

  1. Panelist LeAndra L. loves heading to Disney’s Hollywood Studios with her little ones for a Halloween-inspired character dining experience at Hollywood & Vine. “Minnie and her friends come out to play in their Halloween best while your family enjoys a yummy, buffet-style meal. Not only will your kiddos love giggling with Cowboy Goofy and a not-so-scary Vampire Mickey, but little ones under the age of 3 eat for free at All-You-Care-To-Enjoy dining experiences!” 
  1. Did you know you can embark on a seasonal scavenger hunt around EPCOT? Panelist Kristi R. says, “Pluto’s Pumpkin Pursuit is a fun addition to your visit that the whole family will enjoy! Simply purchase a game board and stickers, then keep an eye out for hidden character pumpkins around the park.  At the end of your day, you can return your game board for a spooky surprise that makes for a wonderful keepsake!” You can join the hunt September 27 through November 1st while supplies last.

Mickey’s Not-So-Scary Halloween Party  

  1. If you’re heading to Mickey’s Not-So-Scary Halloween Party with little ones, panelist Jennifer S. has some advice for making the most of your evening at this separately ticketed event. “Eat an early dinner and arrive at 4 p.m. to start taking advantage of your time at Magic Kingdom Park. Most little ones won’t make it to midnight (they turn into pumpkins!), so get your family photos taken as soon as you arrive and plan to watch the earlier Mickey’s Boo-To-You Halloween Parade at 8:15 p.m.” 
  1. Panelist Lizzie B. wants to let you in on a little secret. “Adults are allowed to visit Magic Kingdom Park in costume during Mickey’s Not-So-Scary Halloween Party! This makes it the practically perfect opportunity to get the entire family in on the magic by choosing a group costume that’s themed to your little one’s favorite Disney character! Whether you transform together into Andy’s favorite toys or a crew of Never Land locals, be sure to review the event’s official costume guidelines before your visit.” 

Disney Springs 

  1. Panelist Heather S. says Disney Springs is the best place for the whole family to get into the Halloween spirit! “As you stroll through Disney Springs, you’ll find seasonal décor, Halloween merchandise, and delicious treats with a dash of Halloween cheer. In the evening, you’ll experience eerie entertainment like dance parties, street performers, and DJs spinning spooky tunes!” 
  1. Looking for a unique way to commemorate your family’s visit to Walt Disney World during the Halloween season? Panelist Alison S. recommends stopping by the Disney PhotoPass Studio at Disney Springs for a seasonal photo session. “The Disney PhotoPass photographers will be as happy as a grim-grinning ghost to capture your Halloween memories! There’s no cost for the photo experience and no reservations are required. Your downloads are available for viewing and purchase on the My Disney Experience mobile app (and included with your purchase of Memory Maker!)” 

Disney Resorts Collection 

  1. After a long day at the theme parks, panelist Betsy S. and her family love to head back to their Disney Resort hotel to catch a movie under the stars. “As Halloween creeps closer, be sure to check the recreation schedule to see which slightly spooky flicks are showing during your stay. Stop by your resort’s quick-service dining location to pick up some seasonal treats and set up for a frightfully fun evening!”  
  1. If you’re staying at a Disney Resort hotel on Halloween night, panelist Lauren H. recommends visiting the front desk or checking out the special activities board to see what Halloween-inspired activities your resort has planned. “One year, my kids got to put on their Halloween costumes and join a parade in the lobby. You might also find trick-or-treat trails, haunted scavenger hunts, ghoulish pool parties, and more!”  

Well, there you have it! If you have any Disney vacation planning questions, stop by planDisney.com to ask one of our fang-tastic panelists! You can also follow along @planDisneyPanel on Instagram for more vacation planning tips and insights. Happy haunting!

Get those ears on, charge those laptops, and get ready to apply for the 2025 planDisney Panel! That’s right, it is time for the planDisney Search to begin, and we can’t wait to hear from guests just like you who would like to apply to be a part of planDisney. 

This year’s search will open at noon ET on Monday, July 8, 2024, and close promptly at noon ET on Saturday, July 13, 2024. Through this process we are looking for guests who enjoy sharing their Disney vacation planning knowledge and excellent tips with others. A master Disney vacation planner if you will. Does this sound like you? If so, then apply to be a 2025 planDisney Panelist and join our adventure.

What is planDisney? 

That is a great question! planDisney is a diverse group of passionate and knowledgeable guests who share their insights on planDisney.com to help other guests maximize their Disney vacations. Panelists are chosen for their ability to efficiently answer guests’ questions in one of four specialties: Walt Disney World Resort, Disneyland Resort, Disney Cruise Line or Disney Vacation Club. Specialist panelists even answer questions in four languages: English, Spanish, French and Portuguese.

“For Guests, planDisney is a place to get information and advice from people like them who want to create memories with families and friends at a Disney Destination,” explains planDisney Panelist Jana. “For panelists, it is a rare chance as an adult to meet a new large group of friends. I expected I would meet fabulous people, but what I didn’t expect is just how important they would be in my life. Sharing our experiences, our stories, our love of all things Disney makes answering questions fun; knowing these individuals makes life magical!” 

How do I become a 2025 planDisney panelist? 

The search for new panelists is an application process that consists of three rounds: a series of short answer questions, a brief video submission and a virtual interview. You can begin your application adventure on Monday, July 8 at noon ET. Whether this is your first time applying or your seventeenth, be sure to visit planDisneySearch.com for more information and eligibility requirements.

What makes a great panelist? 

Another great question and one that could garner many different responses, but at the end of the day, what we are truly searching for are individuals who are determined to share their knowledge, experience, and passion for helping other guests plan the most magical vacations possible.  

“I think one of my favorite parts of being a planDisney panelist has been helping people from all over the world as they plan their next trip to the Disneyland Resort,” said Greg L. “I live a whole state away (in Arizona) from the Happiest Place on Earth so there is some extra planning that comes with having to make the trek to sunny California. However, that’s nothing compared to an international flight from a completely different country! It’s been my absolute pleasure to help our international guests as they navigate a trip to a different country.” 

What is it like to be a panelist? 

Being a member of planDisney is a rewarding experience in that our panelists are able to help other guests plan their Disney dream vacation. It requires dedicated time each week to answer questions and may often involve research on the panelists’ part. Their work is done from home via their own device, and they must meet a weekly quota. 

“One of my favorite aspects of serving as a planDisney panelist is how it has reminded me of why I love Disney destinations so deeply,” explains panelist Lizzie. “The opportunity to hear the experiences of my incredible fellow panelists and discuss our favorite magical memories regularly reminds me that everyone’s vacations to Disney destinations are unique and that there isn’t just one way to make magic. As a panelist, I’ve even been inspired to try new experiences, visit new Disney destinations, and embrace new family traditions in The Most Magical Place on Earth.” 

Does all of this sound like you? If so, we look forward to hearing from you for the 2025 planDisney Search! While you wait for applications to open, we encourage you to study up on what planDisney is all about and what it takes to be a panelist: 

  • Visit the About planDisney page to learn more about the program.
  • Meet the current panelists on the Meet the Panel page.
  • Stay tuned to @planDisneypanel on Instagram as the current panelists share what it’s like to be a planDisney panelist and give insights on the Search process.
  • Check out the special episode of the planDisney Podcast where panelists share their personal application stories and tips for applying. 

Best of luck to all those applying. Just remember to mark your calendar! Search starts noon ET on Monday, July 8, 2024, and closes promptly at noon ET on Saturday, July 13, 2024. And if we could give you one piece of advice…relax and just be yourself!

Pack your bags and get ready to check in to the Hotels of the Disneyland Resort on this month’s episode of the planDisney Podcast! There are three hotels at Disneyland Resort, and each offers a truly unique vacation experience.  

Not sure which hotel is best for your travel party? Join us on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, or wherever you get your podcasts to hear panelists Amanda D. and Prince S. break down the offerings at each hotel – from theming and room types to dining and recreation.  

Why Stay at Disneyland Resort? 

Staying at one of the Hotels of the Disneyland Resort is the key to maximizing the magic. Amanda loves that all three hotels are a quick walk to and from the theme parks and the Downtown Disney District, making it easy and convenient to get around with her little ones.  

For Prince, it’s all about early entry!* Entering a designated theme park 30 minutes early allows him to get a head start on his day at the park and experience select attractions and more before the park opens to day guests.  

Tune into the podcast to hear about even more special perks for hotel guests, and check out the events calendar to see what activities and entertainment offerings are happening during your visit. 

Experience Animated Artistry at Pixar Place Hotel  

The first and only Pixar-themed hotel in the United States is now open! The panelists share an overview of all there is to see and do when you stay at Pixar Place Hotel. Amanda is excited to grab a sandwich from Small Bytes to enjoy by the “Finding Nemo”-inspired pool deck, while Prince is eager to sign up for a Learn to Draw class and embrace his inner artist. 

As you explore all this hotel has to offer, keep an eye out for familiar Pixar friends! You might hear Joe Gardner playing soulful tunes in the lobby or catch our favorite imaginary friend Bing Bong making core memories up on the rooftop lawn!  

Celebrate Disneyland Past and Present at Disneyland Hotel  

If you love Disney parks history like Prince, you’ll love staying at Disneyland Hotel! The panelists share how this hotel pays homage to the classic years of Disneyland park, from whimsical rooms and suites down to the Monorail-themed slides and the E-Ticket pool.  

The Villas at Disneyland Hotel, the new Disney Vacation Club tower at Disneyland Hotel, is home to a variety of room types inspired by classic Disney films. This new addition is a favorite among guests and Members alike and opened with new amenities like the Palm Breeze Bar, which Amanda says is the perfect place to grab a quick bite or refreshing beverage. 

Relax in Craftsman-Style Elegance at Disney’s Grand Californian Hotel & Spa  

Disney’s Grand Californian Hotel & Spa is the perfect place to relax and recharge after a day in the theme parks. The panelists share a glimpse of the Craftsman-style rooms and suites, exquisite dining experiences and peaceful pool areas at this hotel. 

Amanda says the Tenaya Stone Spa is not to be missed. Treat yourself to a signature spa service or sign up for one of the unique fitness class offerings. Did someone say sunrise yoga in Disneyland park? You’ll have to tune in to find out!
