Disney Eats: Foodie Guide to EPCOT International Food & Wine Festival 2024

Published: August 13, 2024

Contributed By

Nicole Cantore

Digital Integration Specialist

Foodies get ready because the time to taste your way around the world is almost here. The countdown to the EPCOT International Food & Wine Festival presented by CORKCICLE is fast approaching. This delicious celebration takes place from Aug. 29 through Nov. 23, 2024, and is filled with incredibly tasty offerings featuring cuisines from around the globe.

Hopefully you’ve saved your appetite because we have plenty of new additions and classic items on the menu this year. From the Liquid Nitrogen Frozen Sweet Potato Mousse at the Swirled Showcase to the Tostada de Camarones at Mexico, you’ll embark on a flavorful journey. 

Don’t forget to savor the flavors with one of the many signature drinks such as the refreshing Blood Orange Agua Fresca from Earth Eats. No matter where your destination takes you, there are tons of delicious bites and sips for your taste buds to celebrate. 

As you embark on your flavorful adventure, you’ll want to check out Emile’s Fromage Montage. Start planning your cheesy stroll with the menu items designated with a 🧀 below. 

The return of this fan-favorite food stroll features several cheesy goodies from the Global Marketplaces. Simply pick up a Festival Passport and collect stamps by purchasing five of these items. Once you’ve gotten them all, head over to Shimmering Sips presented by CORKCICLE for a special redemption prize.

New this year is the exclusive Disney Gift Card wristlet artwork showcasing Mickey Mouse and Minnie Mouse taking over the kitchen to create culinary masterpieces. These delightful cards are available at merchandise locations throughout EPCOT during the festival. Use them to pay easily at Global Marketplaces, merchandise locations, and anywhere Disney Gift Card is accepted!
